<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><i><b>Energy for Sustainable Development</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peer-reviewed" title="Peer-reviewed">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Academic_journal" title="Academic journal">academic journal</a> covering research on <a href="/wiki/Energy" title="Energy">energy</a>-related aspects of <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_development" title="Sustainable development">sustainable development</a>. It is published by <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is Daniel B. Jones. According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2013 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 2.360, ranking it 37th out of 83 journals in the category "Energy &amp; Fuels".</p>
