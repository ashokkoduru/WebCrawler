<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Society for Organizational Learning</b> (<b>SoL</b>) is an American organization founded in 1997 by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Senge" title="Peter Senge">Peter Senge</a>. It replaced the Center for <a href="/wiki/Organizational_learning" title="Organizational learning">Organizational Learning</a> at <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts_Institute_of_Technology" title="Massachusetts Institute of Technology">MIT</a>. Since 1999, SoL publishes its own journal, "Reflections". Its European Counterpart is the <b>European Consortium for the Learning Organisation</b> (<b>ECLO</b>), established in Brussels in 1990. It was originally founded by several European multinationals as a response to the US orientated approach of Peter Senge with a special emphasis to the European complexity of regions, nations, history, tradition, etc. Its Journal "The Learner" and its electronic version "eLearner" are among the oldest publications in this field.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sustainability_Consortium">Sustainability Consortium</span></h2>
<p>SoL has a "Sustainability Consortium" which helps large corporations including <a href="/wiki/Unilever" title="Unilever">Unilever</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coca-Cola" title="Coca-Cola">Coca-Cola</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seventh_Generation_Inc." title="Seventh Generation Inc.">Seventh Generation</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Schlumberger" title="Schlumberger">Schlumberger</a> share ideas and tackle common issues related to <a href="/wiki/Sustainability" title="Sustainability">sustainability</a>. The ECLO is regularly cooperating with <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=EU_project&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="EU project (page does not exist)">EU projects</a> to promote the idea of sustainable learning, conceptualizing projects, and disseminating its results.</p>
