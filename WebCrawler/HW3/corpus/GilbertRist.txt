<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><b>Gilbert Rist</b> was an honorary professor at the <a href="/wiki/Graduate_Institute_of_International_and_Development_Studies" title="Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies">Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies</a> in <a href="/wiki/Geneva" title="Geneva">Geneva</a> and a thought leader of <a href="/wiki/Postdevelopment_theory" title="Postdevelopment theory">postdevelopment theory</a>. He is best known for his ground-breaking study, <i>The History of Development: From Western Origins to Global Faith</i>, which criticizes <a href="/wiki/Human_development_(humanity)" title="Human development (humanity)">development</a>.</p>
<p>He holds a PhD from the Graduate Institute of International Studies.</p>
