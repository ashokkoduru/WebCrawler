<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p>The <b>Lower Snake River Wind Project</b> is under construction in Washington’s <a href="/wiki/Garfield_County,_Washington" title="Garfield County, Washington">Garfield</a> and <a href="/wiki/Columbia_County,_Washington" title="Columbia County, Washington">Columbia counties</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Wind_farm" title="Wind farm">wind farm</a> is being developed in phases with the first phase consisting of 149 Siemens <a href="/wiki/Wind_turbine" title="Wind turbine">wind turbines</a> rated at 2.3 MW each for a maximum generating capacity of 343 MW. The project will also create up to 150 temporary jobs and the installation will create up to 25 permanent jobs.</p>
