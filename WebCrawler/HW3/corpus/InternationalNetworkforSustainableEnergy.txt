<p>The <b>International Network for Sustainable Energy</b> (<b>INFORSE</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/International" title="International">international</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Non-profit_organisation" title="Non-profit organisation">non-profit organisation</a> (NGO) network of <a href="/wiki/Environmental_organization" title="Environmental organization">environmental organizations</a> promoting <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_energy" title="Sustainable energy">sustainable energy</a> to protect <a href="/wiki/Environment_(biophysical)" title="Environment (biophysical)">the environment</a> and to decrease <a href="/wiki/Poverty" title="Poverty">poverty</a>.</p>
<p></p>

<p></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activities">Activities</span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Develops scenarios for a transition to a 100% renewables energy supply with factor four <a href="/wiki/Efficient_energy_use" title="Efficient energy use">energy efficiency</a> by 2050. A global, EU-27 and European national scenarios are developed. The name of the sustainable energy model is <i>INFORSE Vision 2050</i>.,</li>
<li>World-wide database of about 1000 contacts, web based resources on EU policy, school materials, successes</li>
<li>Online course material (DIERET)</li>
<li>Quarterly newsletter: Sustainable Energy News (SEN) published since 1992</li>
<li>Participation on UN Conferences as NGO observer as INFORSE has <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Consultative_Status" title="Consultative Status">Consultative Status</a> at UN <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ECOSOC" title="ECOSOC">ECOSOC</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UNFCCC" title="UNFCCC">UNFCCC</a>. Participation at e.g.,:

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UNFCCC" title="UNFCCC">UNFCCC</a> (e.g., Exhibition and side event at COP 14, Poznan, 2008; and COP 15 Copenhagen</li>
<li>Beijing International Renewable Energy Conference (BIREC 2005), Beijing, China</li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span></h2>
<p>INFORSE was established in 1992 at the Global Forum, which was a parallel forum to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UNCED" title="UNCED">UNCED</a> Conference so called <a href="/wiki/Earth_Summit" title="Earth Summit">Earth Summit</a>. In 1998, INFORSE got consultative status to United Nation's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ECOSOC" title="ECOSOC">ECOSOC</a>. INFORSE is working through Regional Coordinators in Asia, Africa, Europe, Americas. The INFORSE Secretariat is in Denmark. In 2002, <a href="/wiki/INFORSE-Europe" title="INFORSE-Europe">INFORSE-Europe</a> was registered in Denmark as an NGO network working on European level.</p>
