<p>This is a <b>list of <a href="/wiki/Multilateral_environmental_agreement" title="Multilateral environmental agreement">international environmental agreements</a></b>.</p>
<p>Most of the following agreements are legally binding for countries that have formally ratified them. Some, such as the <a href="/wiki/Kyoto_Protocol" title="Kyoto Protocol">Kyoto Protocol</a>, differentiate between types of countries and each nation's respective responsibilities under the agreement.</p>
