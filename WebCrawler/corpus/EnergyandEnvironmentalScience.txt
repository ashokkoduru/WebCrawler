<p><i><b>Energy and Environmental Science</b></i> is a monthly <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> publishing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Original_research" title="Original research">original (primary) research</a> and <a href="/wiki/Literature_review" title="Literature review">review articles</a>. The journal covers work of an interdisciplinary nature in the biochemical and biophysical sciences and chemical engineering disciplines. <i>Energy &amp; Environmental Science</i> is published by the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Society_of_Chemistry" title="Royal Society of Chemistry">Royal Society of Chemistry</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is Nathan Lewis (<a href="/wiki/California_Institute_of_Technology" title="California Institute of Technology">California Institute of Technology</a>).</p>
<p>According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2015 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 25.427, ranking it 4th out of 163 journals in the category "Chemistry, Multidisciplinary", first out of 88 journals in the category "Energy &amp; Fuels", first out of 135 journals in the category "Engineering, Chemical", and first among 225 journals in the category "Environmental Sciences".</p>
<p></p>

<p></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Article_types">Article types</span></h2>
<p><i>Energy &amp; Environmental Science</i> publishes the following types of articles: Research Papers (original scientific work); Review Articles, Perspectives, and Minireviews (feature review-type articles of broad interest); Communications (original scientific work of an urgent nature), Opinions (personal, often speculative, viewpoints or hypotheses on a current topic), and Analysis Articles (in-depth examination of energy and environmental technologies, strategies, policies, and general conceptual frameworks of general interest).</p>
