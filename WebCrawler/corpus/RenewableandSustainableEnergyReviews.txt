<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><i><b>Renewable and Sustainable Energy Reviews</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peer-review" title="Peer-review">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> covering research on <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_energy" title="Sustainable energy">sustainable energy</a>. It is published in 12 issues per year by <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is L. Kazmerski (<a href="/wiki/National_Renewable_Energy_Laboratory" title="National Renewable Energy Laboratory">National Renewable Energy Laboratory</a>). According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2015 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 6.798.</p>
