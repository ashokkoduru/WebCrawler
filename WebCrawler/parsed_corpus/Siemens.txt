ˈziːmɛns is a german company headquartered in berlin and munich and the largest engineering company in europe with branch offices abroad the principal divisions of the company are industry energy healthcare and infrastructure cities which represent the main activities of the company the company is a prominent maker of medical diagnostics equipment and its medical health-care division which halske was founded by werner von siemens and johann georg halske on 12 october 1847 based on the telegraph his invention used a needle to point to the sequence of letters instead of using morse code
